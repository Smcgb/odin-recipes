Cheese Pizza

Description

A classic cheese pizza, without garlic

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1 cup Fody pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Romano cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). If you are baking the pizza on a pizza stone, you may place your toppings on the pizza dough, and bake immediately. If you are baking your pizza in a pan, lightly oil the pan, and let the dough rise for 15 or 20 minutes before topping and baking it.
  2. Spread pizza sauce over dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over sauce. Spread Parmesan cheese over mozzarella, and distribute small amounts of Romano cheese over the top.
  4. Bake pizza in preheated oven, until the cheese and crust are golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes.
